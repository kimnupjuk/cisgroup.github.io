Asian Journal of Organic Chemistry

Weinheim, Germany. March 26, 2012 Wiley-VCH, part of the scientific and technical publishing business of John Wiley & Sons, Inc., and the Asian Chemical Editorial Society (ACES) today announced the launch of the Asian Journal of Organic Chemistry, the second pan-Asia society chemistry journal after ChemistryAn Asian Journal (2010 Impact Factor: 4.188), launched by ACES and Wiley-VCH in 2006.

For the publication of the Asian Journal of Organic Chemistry, ACES is joined by the Korean Society of Organic Synthesis.
